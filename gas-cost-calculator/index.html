💡 Fuel Saving Tips
  • Keep tires properly inflated — under-inflated tires reduce MPG by up to 3%
  • Drive at a steady speed — using cruise control on highways saves up to 14% fuel
  • Avoid aggressive acceleration and hard braking — smooth driving improves efficiency
  • Remove excess weight from your trunk — every 100 lbs reduces MPG by about 1%
  • Use the recommended grade of motor oil for your vehicle
  • Plan your route to avoid traffic — idling wastes fuel at 0 MPG
